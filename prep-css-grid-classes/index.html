Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Harrison Ford plays an archaeologist vying with Nazi forces in 1936 to recover the Ark of the Covenant, racing to stop a rival archaeologist from guiding Nagit zis to the Ark and its power.

Temple of Doom

Harrison Ford reprises his role as the title character, asked by desperate villagers in India to find a mystical stone and rescue their children from a Thuggee cult practicing child slavery, black magic, and human sacrifice in honor of the goddess Kali.

Last Crusade

Harrison Ford reprises his role as the title character, searching for his father, a Holy Grail scholar played by Sean Connery, who has been kidnapped by Nazis.
